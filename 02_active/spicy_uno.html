Spicy UNO

Have Fun and Play Spicy!

Base UNO Rules

Setup & Gameplay

  • Each player starts with 7 cards.
  • Match cards by color or number to discard.
  • Say "UNO" when you have one card left. First player to discard all cards wins.
  • If another player notices you have one card left and you forgot to say "UNO", they may call you out by saying "UNO" before you. You must draw 2 cards as a penalty.

Special Cards

Skip: Next player skips their turn.

Reverse: Play direction changes.

Draw Two: Next player draws 2 cards and skips their turn.

Wild: Change the color to any color.

Wild Draw Four: Change color and next player draws 4 cards.

Spicy UNO Additions

Starting the Game

  • Anyone may start the game by playing a valid card on top of the starting card from the draw pile.
  • Once the first card is placed, play continues clockwise from that player.

Jump-Ins

If you have an exact match (same color and number) as the top card, you may play it out of turn, skipping players ahead of you.

Stacking

  • Once any + card (+2 or +4) is played, players can begin stacking out of turn. Each stack increases the draw total for the next player.
  • +4 cards may be placed on top of +2 cards, but +2 cards may not be placed on top of +4s.
  • A +4 can jump in to stack on either a +2 or another +4.
Number-Specific Spicy Rules

0 - Rotate Hands

Everyone passes their hand in the direction of play.

1 - Trade Hands

Trade hands with any player. Play pauses until you decide. You may choose not to trade.

6 - Table Slap

Everyone slaps the table. Last to slap draws 1 card.

7 - No Talking

No one is allowed to talk until another 7 is played. Anytime a player talks, that player draws 1 card.

Silent UNO Rule

  • If No Talking is in effect (after a 7 is played), you must raise your index finger instead of saying "UNO".
  • If you say "UNO" out loud while silence is in effect, you must draw 1 card.

If You Have No Card to Play

Option 1 - Draw Until You Can Play:
You must draw from the draw pile until you draw a playable card.

Option 2 - Ask for Help:
You may ask another player for a card.

  • They cannot show you the card before you accept it. If they do so, you are NOT allowed to accept the card.
  • Once the card is in your possession (even if you haven't looked at it), you must take it and add it to your hand.
  • Players ARE allowed to lie about the card they give you.
