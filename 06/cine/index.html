How to Play: Original

16 clue tiles are drawn from 4 hidden films. Swap them into rows so each row contains clues from exactly one film.

  • Click two tiles to swap them
  • A row glows yellow when 3 of 4 tiles match one film
  • A row locks green when all 4 match
  • You have 20 swaps — more remaining = higher score
  • Watch for red herrings: one attribute might apply to two films!

How to Play: Reversal

16 movie titles are shuffled. Group them into rows — each row shares a hidden theme.

  • Click two tiles to swap them
  • Possible themes: director, lead actor, genre, decade, country, runtime
  • Locked rows reveal their theme
  • You have 20 swaps

How to Play: Burgle

5 mystery films are locked in vaults. Crack each one for points!

  • Click "Next Clue" to reveal clues one by one
  • Type your guess at any time and press Enter or click Guess
  • Score = 7 − clues revealed per vault (max 35 total)
  • 3 wrong guesses = vault cracked, 0 points
  • Matching ignores "The/A/An" and is case-insensitive
